Effects Gas Consumption

The average person drives their car to work, go around the corner, or travel a short distance. The amount of gas we use can add up relatively fast. This can greatly dammage the environment as it produces more greenhouse gases, leading to more global warming.

We can reduce this by:

  • Walking more
  • Investing in an electric car
  • Carpooling

Effects Of Electricity

The main issue is that the production of electricity also produces a big amount of CO2. This goes back to the previous point of global warmimg.

We can reduce this by:

  • Turning off the lights in unused rooms
  • Investing in solar panels

Effects Of Plastic

Plastic has come to be very useful, but the problem is that it stays around for generations upon generations. This is terrible as we keep producing more and more, but can't get rid of it at the same rate. The plastic gets thrown into the ocean and hurts the ecosystem; small plastic particles float around us and get into our lungs; and the production of plastic also generates CO2.

We can reduce this by:

  • Using fewer plastic products
  • Use reusable bags instead of plastic bags
  • The plastic that does get used, cut it so other organisms can't get stuck
  • Recylce to reduce the amount of waste
